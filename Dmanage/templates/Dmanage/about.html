About Us:

Our idea is a crowdsourced disaster reporting tool that aims to improve real-time disaster awareness and response. It allows users to report crucial circumstances like: fires, floods, earthquakes, and more via a web interface, ensuring that accurate and timely information is shared with emergency services and the general public. The platform's spam filtering and verification method demands numerous reports from the same area before publishing, thereby reducing false alarms and ensuring only trustworthy data is posted. Once validated, reports are displayed on an interactive dashboard, providing a clear, real-time snapshot of disaster activities. Simultaneously, rapid alerts are delivered to users in affected areas and emergency services, allowing for quick action to limit damage and save lives. Our technology helps emergency services make faster, more informed decisions, eventually boosting community safety and optimizing emergency response efforts when they are most required.
